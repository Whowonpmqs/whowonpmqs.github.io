What Is This?

Who Won PMQs? is an independent public voting app. Every Wednesday, anyone in the UK can rate the performance of politicians at Prime Minister's Questions and vote on who they think won.

Completely non-partisan. No political affiliation. Results driven entirely by public votes, not editorial opinion.
